Self Introduction

I currently reading for master in financial analysis at LBS and wonder to pursue my future career in the finance industry. I breaked into the industry and ensured my first internship at Sierra Madre Group by enhancing interpersonal skills and sending hundreds of cold mails and calls. From this experience, I was able to have a glimpse of how finance help to provide operating budgets and revenue forecasts for management teams of companies. Then, I joined Institutional Capital Advisory and focused more on client-facing jobs. By arranging road shows, financial news releases and annual shareholder meetings, I had success in leading my client which is a well-known fashion brand in China to increase visibility. After these two internships, I realized that well roundedness is probably the best word to describe a successful financial professional. The holistic approach could sometimes be the best alternative. I put this discipline into my next two internships at Affinity Management and Pivoton Capital. I had an excellent opportunity to develop my decision-making by working on strategic deals like the acquisition of one Canadian software company.Furthermore, I reassured myself that finance industry would be my destination.
